Nigma Galaxy (formerly known as Team Nigma) is a European esports organization founded by former Team Liquid members and four out of five of the Champions of The International 2017.

Nigma Galaxy timeline

Nigma Galaxy (formerly known as Team Nigma) is a European esports organization founded by former Team Liquid members and four out of five of the Champions of International 2017. After finishing 2nd at The International 2019, captain KuroKy announced the team's departure from Team Liquid with the idea of forming their own organization.

  • 2019 November 25th - The team is announced, consisting of members of the former Team Liquid roster: Miracle-, w33, MinD_ContRoL, GH and KuroKy.[1]
  • 2021 April 6th - w33 is moved from the active roster and becomes the 6th player.[3]
  • 2021 April 10th - iLTW joins the team.[4]
  • 2021 September 20th - The organization merge with the competitive division of Galaxy Racer to form Nigma Galaxy.[5]
  • Amer "Miracle-" Al-Barkawi is a Jordanian/Polish professional Dota 2 player who is playing for Nigma Galaxy.
  • Igor "iLTW" Filatov is a Russian professional Dota 2 player who is currently playing for Nigma Galaxy.
  • van Borislavov "MinD_ContRoL" Ivanov is a Bulgarian professional Dota 2 player who is playing for Nimga Galaxy.
  • Maroun "GH" Merhej is a Lebanese professional Dota 2 player who is playing for Nigma Galaxy.
  • uro "KuroKy" (pronounced kuroKy) Salehi Takhasomi is a professional Dota 2 player who is playing for Nigma Galaxy.
